# Search Plan

## Search Surfaces

- Global site search
- Product and family discovery
- Insight and case-study discovery
- Market and corridor discovery
- FAQ and trust-content discovery

## Launch Strategy

- Build a lightweight internal search index from published Sanity content and route metadata.
- Support prefix and keyword matching for product names, sectors, industries, markets, and insight topics.
- Use canonical content pages as the ranking destination rather than generating separate thin landing pages.

## Search UX Rules

- Search must be accessible from the global header and dedicated `/search` page.
- Results must be grouped by content type where helpful.
- Empty states should suggest sectors, markets, or insights instead of dead ends.
- Search must work well on mobile without hover-only interactions.

## Index Scope

- Product families and products
- Industries and capabilities
- Markets, origins, destinations, and trade corridors
- Insight articles and case studies
- FAQ and trust-center documents

## Deferred Decisions

- Dedicated search provider
- Personalized ranking
- Synonym and typo-tolerance sophistication beyond launch needs

## Validation Rules

- Confirm representative queries across all major content types.
- Validate search payload freshness after CMS publish.
- Ensure search results never expose unpublished draft content to public users.
