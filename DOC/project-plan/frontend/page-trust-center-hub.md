# Trust Center Hub Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/trust-center`
- Template type: trust and policy hub
- Primary audiences: buyers, suppliers, due-diligence reviewers
- Primary CTA: Contact Compliance Or Commercial Team
- Secondary CTA: Download Relevant Documentation

## Goal

Centralize proof assets that reduce procurement friction and speed up due diligence.

## Section Plan

### 1. Hero
- Purpose: Position the hub as a due-diligence destination.
- Content: summary of what is available and why it matters.

### 2. Certificates And Registrations
- Purpose: Surface formal proof.
- Content: certificates, memberships, registrations.

### 3. Policy Library
- Purpose: Surface governance and compliance docs.
- Content: privacy, sanctions, anti-bribery, supplier code, ESG pages.

### 4. Documentation Standards
- Purpose: Explain trade-document expectations.
- Content: inspection, shipping, QA, and supporting paperwork overview.

### 5. FAQ Block
- Purpose: Address recurring trust questions.
- Content: payment terms, QA, origin documents, dispute posture.

### 6. Contact And Escalation CTA
- Purpose: Provide human follow-up path.
- Content: commercial and compliance contact options.

## State Coverage

- Default: trust asset library.
- Loading: document-card skeletons.
- Empty: if no downloadable assets are published, keep policy and FAQ sections active.
- Success: tracked download acknowledgement if needed.
- Error: fallback to contact route and visible policy summaries.
