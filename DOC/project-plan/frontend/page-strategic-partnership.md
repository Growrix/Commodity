# Strategic Partnership Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/partner-with-us/partners`
- Template type: conversion page
- Primary audiences: logistics, storage, finance, distribution, regional partners
- Primary CTA: Start Partnership Discussion
- Secondary CTA: Explore Capabilities

## Goal

Clarify which strategic partnerships matter to the business and capture qualified partnership interest without vague generic outreach.

## Section Plan

### 1. Hero
- Purpose: Define the partnership posture.
- Content: partnership summary, who should engage, CTA.

### 2. Partnership Models
- Purpose: Explain relevant lanes.
- Content: logistics, warehousing, finance, market-entry, regional distribution.

### 3. What We Need From Partners
- Purpose: Self-qualify inbound interest.
- Content: coverage, expertise, infrastructure, compliance expectations.

### 4. Partnership Inquiry Form
- Purpose: Capture the right business context.
- Content: company, region, service model, capabilities, attachments, message.

### 5. Proof And Trust
- Purpose: Show that the company is a credible counterparty.
- Content: markets served, operational strengths, trust assets.

### 6. FAQ
- Purpose: Clarify review and response model.
- Content: timelines, confidentiality, diligence expectations.

## State Coverage

- Default: partnership qualification page.
- Loading: form skeletons.
- Empty: not applicable.
- Success: acknowledgement with next-step expectations.
- Error: validation and upload retry states.
