# Home Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/`
- Template type: static marketing root
- Primary audiences: buyers, suppliers, strategic partners
- Primary CTA: Request a Quote
- Secondary CTA: Explore Products

## Goal

Establish institutional trust immediately, communicate commodity breadth, and route visitors into the correct commercial journey.

## Section Plan

### 1. Hero
- Purpose: Clarify global trading capability and commercial reliability.
- Content: headline, support copy, two CTAs, quick proof stats.
- Components: hero copy block, CTA group, stat strip.
- Responsive notes: stats collapse into a swipeable or stacked layout on mobile.

### 2. Trust Strip
- Purpose: Reduce perceived procurement risk.
- Content: certifications, compliance claims, regions served, sectors traded.
- Components: logo strip, compliance badges, proof cards.

### 3. Sector Overview
- Purpose: Route users into Agriculture, Energy, Fertilizers, and Industrial Materials.
- Content: sector cards with one-line summary and key products.
- Components: four-up sector grid, hover or focus states, mobile carousel fallback.

### 4. Why Work With Us
- Purpose: Translate credibility into operational strengths.
- Content: sourcing network, QA, logistics, documentation discipline, market intelligence.
- Components: icon-led capability cards.

### 5. Trade Corridor Snapshot
- Purpose: Make global reach tangible.
- Content: origin-destination highlights, port access, region coverage.
- Components: corridor map or structured route cards.

### 6. Capability Snapshot
- Purpose: Show end-to-end commercial execution.
- Content: origination, trade finance, logistics, quality assurance, risk management.
- Components: capability cards with deep links.

### 7. Featured Insights
- Purpose: Support authority and SEO-driven conversion.
- Content: latest insights, market updates, or case proof.
- Components: article cards, section header, link to insights hub.

### 8. Segmented Conversion Band
- Purpose: Split buyers, suppliers, and partners into the right path.
- Content: three CTA panels with audience-specific microcopy.
- Components: CTA band, contextual buttons.

### 9. Footer
- Purpose: Close with contact, trust, and utility access.
- Content: office info, legal links, trust links, newsletter sign-up if retained.
- Components: global footer.

## State Coverage

- Default: full marketing presentation.
- Loading: skeleton only for dynamic insight cards if data is streamed.
- Empty: hide featured insights module if no featured content exists; preserve CTA band.
- Success: not applicable beyond successful newsletter capture if included.
- Error: show safe fallback content if insights or corridor data fail to load.

## SEO And Conversion Notes

- Must support organization schema and top-level navigation schema.
- Above-the-fold content must establish trust before asking for conversion.

## Dependencies

- Global navigation and footer modules from CMS.
- Featured insights and corridor highlights from CMS.
