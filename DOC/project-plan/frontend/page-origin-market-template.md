# Origin Market Template Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/markets/origins/[slug]`
- Template type: dynamic CMS template
- Primary audiences: buyers, suppliers, partners
- Primary CTA: Discuss Supply From This Origin
- Secondary CTA: Explore Related Products

## Goal

Explain sourcing strength for one origin market, including commodity availability, seasonality, logistics, and local trade context.

## Section Plan

### 1. Hero
- Purpose: Define the origin market and its strategic value.
- Content: region summary, flagship commodities, trade context.

### 2. Commodity Availability
- Purpose: Show what the market is known for.
- Content: families, grades, seasonal timing.

### 3. Supplier Ecosystem
- Purpose: Build sourcing confidence.
- Content: sourcing approach, partner base, QA controls.

### 4. Logistics And Port Access
- Purpose: Connect origin to fulfillment reality.
- Content: ports, inland movement, shipping modes.

### 5. Risks And Compliance Notes
- Purpose: Demonstrate operational realism.
- Content: seasonality, documentation, regulatory notes.

### 6. CTA Block
- Purpose: Route into sourcing conversations.
- Content: request quote, sourcing request, supplier CTA.

## State Coverage

- Default: market-specific sourcing page.
- Loading: hero and route-card skeletons.
- Empty: fallback summary and regional contact CTA.
- Success: not applicable.
- Error: route back to markets hub with fallback contact.
