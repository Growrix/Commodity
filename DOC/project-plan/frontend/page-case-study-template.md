# Case Study Template Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/case-studies/[slug]`
- Template type: dynamic CMS template
- Primary audiences: buyers, partners
- Primary CTA: Discuss A Similar Requirement
- Secondary CTA: Explore Capabilities

## Goal

Use anonymized proof to demonstrate execution quality, corridor expertise, and problem-solving ability without oversharing sensitive client data.

## Section Plan

### 1. Hero
- Purpose: Summarize the business situation and outcome.
- Content: title, sector, region, outcome summary.

### 2. Challenge
- Purpose: Explain the original supply or logistics problem.
- Content: requirement, risk, or market challenge.

### 3. Solution Approach
- Purpose: Show structured execution.
- Content: sourcing, route, QA, documentation, coordination steps.

### 4. Delivery Proof
- Purpose: Build trust.
- Content: metrics, route execution notes, control measures, timeline.

### 5. Related Capabilities And Markets
- Purpose: Route readers deeper.
- Content: capability links, market links, corridor links.

### 6. CTA Footer
- Purpose: Convert proof into inquiry.
- Content: discuss similar requirement, request quote, contact CTA.

## State Coverage

- Default: proof-led case page.
- Loading: hero and metric-card skeletons.
- Empty: not applicable.
- Success: not applicable.
- Error: safe fallback to capabilities hub or insights hub.
