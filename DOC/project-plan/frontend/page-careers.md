# Careers Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/careers`
- Template type: utility brand page
- Primary audiences: job seekers and future team members
- Primary CTA: View Open Roles
- Secondary CTA: Send General Interest

## Goal

Support employer-brand credibility without distracting from the commercial core of the site.

## Section Plan

### 1. Hero
- Purpose: Introduce hiring posture and culture.
- Content: short company and team proposition.

### 2. Why Join Us
- Purpose: Explain the work environment.
- Content: culture, growth, international exposure, commercial impact.

### 3. Open Roles Or Hiring Status
- Purpose: Present active opportunities or an honest no-openings state.
- Content: role cards or general application notice.

### 4. Team And Values
- Purpose: Humanize the brand.
- Content: values, working style, team principles.

### 5. Application CTA
- Purpose: Capture interest.
- Content: role links, hiring contact, general interest route.

## State Coverage

- Default: careers content page.
- Loading: role-card skeletons if roles are dynamic.
- Empty: friendly no-open-roles state with general interest CTA.
- Success: not applicable.
- Error: fallback to company contact and general interest channel.
