# Partner With Us Hub Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/partner-with-us`
- Template type: conversion hub
- Primary audiences: buyers, suppliers, strategic partners
- Primary CTA: Choose Your Path
- Secondary CTA: Contact Trade Desk

## Goal

Segment the three primary commercial audiences into distinct journeys so each sees tailored copy, proof, and qualification paths.

## Section Plan

### 1. Hero
- Purpose: Explain the page as an audience router.
- Content: headline, short explainer, CTA.

### 2. Audience Selector
- Purpose: Split into buyers, suppliers, and strategic partners.
- Content: three cards with role-specific summary and CTA.

### 3. Buyer Path Summary
- Purpose: Show how quote and sourcing flows work.
- Content: product discovery, inquiry model, response expectations.

### 4. Supplier Path Summary
- Purpose: Explain onboarding and document expectations.
- Content: criteria, regions of interest, supplier form link.

### 5. Strategic Partner Path Summary
- Purpose: Clarify what partnerships are relevant.
- Content: logistics, storage, finance, market access, distribution.

### 6. FAQ And Trust Strip
- Purpose: Reduce hesitation before form entry.
- Content: response time, confidentiality, documentation, next steps.

## State Coverage

- Default: segmented conversion hub.
- Loading: not applicable for mostly static routing content.
- Empty: not applicable.
- Success: not applicable.
- Error: degrade to direct contact routing and page-level CTA cards.
