# Legal Template Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/privacy-policy`, `/terms-and-conditions`, `/cookie-policy`
- Template type: reusable legal document template
- Primary audiences: all public visitors
- Primary CTA: Return To Main Site
- Secondary CTA: Contact Responsible Team

## Goal

Present legal content clearly and consistently while preserving readability and traceability.

## Section Plan

### 1. Document Header
- Purpose: Identify the legal page clearly.
- Content: title, effective date, summary, owner.

### 2. Table Of Contents
- Purpose: Improve scanning for long-form legal content.
- Content: in-page anchor links.

### 3. Sectioned Body
- Purpose: Present the legal document.
- Content: structured headings and body copy from CMS or code-owned legal content.

### 4. Related Policy Links
- Purpose: Connect legal pages together.
- Content: privacy, cookie, terms cross-links.

### 5. Contact Footer
- Purpose: Provide a path for follow-up questions.
- Content: responsible contact or general legal inquiry route.

## State Coverage

- Default: full legal document.
- Loading: not applicable for mostly static legal content.
- Empty: unavailable-document fallback with hub or home link.
- Success: not applicable.
- Error: safe fallback and support contact.
