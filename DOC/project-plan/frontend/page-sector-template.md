# Sector Template Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/products/[sector]`
- Template type: dynamic CMS template
- Primary audiences: buyers, suppliers
- Primary CTA: Explore Commodity Families
- Secondary CTA: Request a Quote

## Goal

Explain one sector clearly, organize its commodity families, and connect sector-level commercial context to deeper family or product pages.

## Section Plan

### 1. Sector Hero
- Purpose: Define the sector and its market significance.
- Content: sector description, key use cases, major regions served.

### 2. Family Grid
- Purpose: Route into child commodity families.
- Content: family cards with short descriptors and key products.

### 3. Industry Use Cases
- Purpose: Show how the sector maps to buyer needs.
- Content: industries served, application summaries.

### 4. Origin And Supply Snapshot
- Purpose: Reinforce sourcing credibility.
- Content: common origins, seasonality or supply continuity notes.

### 5. Featured Products Or Specs
- Purpose: Surface top commercial pages from the sector.
- Content: selected products, grades, or market-priority items.

### 6. FAQ
- Purpose: Address sector-level buying questions.
- Content: common grade, logistics, and documentation topics.

### 7. CTA Band
- Purpose: Offer next-step conversion.
- Content: request a quote, sourcing request, talk to team.

## State Coverage

- Default: CMS-driven sector narrative.
- Loading: hero and family-grid skeletons.
- Empty: show fallback CTA and notify that detailed families are being updated.
- Success: not applicable.
- Error: degrade to concise sector summary plus direct inquiry CTA.
