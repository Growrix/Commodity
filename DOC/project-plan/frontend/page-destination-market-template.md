# Destination Market Template Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/markets/destinations/[slug]`
- Template type: dynamic CMS template
- Primary audiences: buyers, partners
- Primary CTA: Request Supply Into This Market
- Secondary CTA: Explore Relevant Commodities

## Goal

Show how the company serves a destination market through relevant commodities, import understanding, and delivery capability.

## Section Plan

### 1. Hero
- Purpose: Define the destination market demand context.
- Content: region summary, industries served, headline opportunity.

### 2. Demand And Use Cases
- Purpose: Show what buyers in this market need.
- Content: industry use cases, demand patterns, key commodities.

### 3. Import And Compliance Framework
- Purpose: Reduce friction for market-entry evaluation.
- Content: documentation expectations, handling norms, compliance notes.

### 4. Delivery Model
- Purpose: Explain how the company supports the market.
- Content: ports, logistics, incoterm posture, response model.

### 5. Related Corridors And Products
- Purpose: Keep discovery connected.
- Content: corridor links, product links, insight links.

### 6. CTA Block
- Purpose: Capture region-specific buying intent.
- Content: quote request, market discussion CTA.

## State Coverage

- Default: destination-market commercial page.
- Loading: market-summary skeletons.
- Empty: fallback to generic destination-market summary and contact CTA.
- Success: not applicable.
- Error: safe route to markets hub.
