# Markets Hub Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/markets`
- Template type: static plus CMS-fed hub
- Primary audiences: buyers, suppliers, partners
- Primary CTA: Explore Trade Corridors
- Secondary CTA: Contact Regional Team

## Goal

Prove geographic coverage through clear market groupings, corridor logic, and logistics credibility.

## Section Plan

### 1. Hero
- Purpose: Frame geographic reach.
- Content: market overview, regions served, corridor summary.

### 2. Global Coverage Map
- Purpose: Visualize origin and destination coverage.
- Content: regions, trade routes, key logistics nodes.

### 3. Origin Market Grid
- Purpose: Route into source-market content.
- Content: origin region cards and key commodities.

### 4. Destination Market Grid
- Purpose: Route into demand-market content.
- Content: destination region cards and buyer industries.

### 5. Trade Corridor Highlights
- Purpose: Show specific route fluency.
- Content: selected corridors with timing and documentation notes.

### 6. Logistics Framework
- Purpose: Reinforce operational execution.
- Content: ports, shipping modes, compliance support.

### 7. CTA Band
- Purpose: Capture region-specific needs.
- Content: discuss destination needs, supplier interest, partnership CTA.

## State Coverage

- Default: map-led market hub.
- Loading: map and card skeletons.
- Empty: show core regions and direct contact CTA.
- Success: not applicable.
- Error: degrade to region list and support contact.
