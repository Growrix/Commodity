# Insights Hub Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/insights`
- Template type: editorial hub
- Primary audiences: buyers, suppliers, researchers, SEO visitors
- Primary CTA: Subscribe For Market Updates
- Secondary CTA: Explore Related Products

## Goal

Build authority and organic growth while routing qualified readers into commercial or subscription actions.

## Section Plan

### 1. Hero
- Purpose: Position insights as market intelligence, not blog filler.
- Content: editorial promise, frequency, topic scope.

### 2. Featured Insights
- Purpose: Surface flagship or timely content.
- Content: lead article and supporting cards.

### 3. Topic Filters
- Purpose: Improve discoverability.
- Content: sector, region, logistics, compliance, market update filters.

### 4. Latest Articles Grid
- Purpose: Support scanning and SEO depth.
- Content: paginated card list or load-more archive.

### 5. Resource Library Highlights
- Purpose: Surface evergreen content.
- Content: guides, spec explainers, documentation primers.

### 6. Subscription Block
- Purpose: Capture nurture leads.
- Content: newsletter form, segmentation options, expectation copy.

### 7. Commercial CTA Strip
- Purpose: Convert intent from content readers.
- Content: quote CTA and explore products CTA.

## State Coverage

- Default: editorial archive.
- Loading: article card skeletons.
- Empty: show featured evergreen resources and subscription CTA.
- Success: subscription confirmation state.
- Error: fallback to featured content only with retry affordance.
