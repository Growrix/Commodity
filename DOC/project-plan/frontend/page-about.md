# About Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/about`
- Template type: static brand and trust page
- Primary audiences: buyers, suppliers, partners, due-diligence reviewers
- Primary CTA: Speak With Our Team
- Secondary CTA: Download Company Profile

## Goal

Turn corporate credibility into a structured, evidence-led explanation of who the company is, how it operates, and why it is trustworthy.

## Section Plan

### 1. Page Hero
- Purpose: Position the company as a serious international trading partner.
- Content: concise brand story, global footprint summary, CTA pair.

### 2. Company Overview
- Purpose: Explain operating model and market focus.
- Content: mission, sectors served, operating philosophy, trade posture.

### 3. Leadership And Governance
- Purpose: Show accountable leadership.
- Content: leadership cards, governance principles, decision-making structure.

### 4. Global Network
- Purpose: Prove reach and corridor familiarity.
- Content: office map, partner network, origin and destination regions.

### 5. Compliance And Ethics
- Purpose: Reduce trust friction.
- Content: sanctions posture, documentation standards, anti-bribery, audit readiness.

### 6. Sustainability And Responsible Sourcing
- Purpose: Address modern procurement expectations.
- Content: sourcing commitments, supplier standards, environmental and social statements.

### 7. Milestones Or Proof Timeline
- Purpose: Show longevity and operational maturity.
- Content: years in market, region expansion, capability milestones.

### 8. Conversion Strip
- Purpose: Route visitors into contact or product discovery.
- Content: request meeting, explore sectors, download company profile.

## State Coverage

- Default: full trust narrative.
- Loading: not applicable for core static content.
- Empty: if leadership or offices are temporarily unavailable, replace with neutral institutional summary.
- Success: download confirmation for profile if gated or tracked.
- Error: show fallback trust copy and direct contact CTA.

## Dependencies

- Leadership, office, and policy content from CMS.
- Download asset managed through CMS or approved asset system.
