# Capabilities Hub Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/capabilities`
- Template type: static plus CMS-fed hub
- Primary audiences: buyers, partners, procurement reviewers
- Primary CTA: Discuss Your Supply Program
- Secondary CTA: Explore Markets

## Goal

Show that the company is an execution partner with structured capabilities across sourcing, logistics, quality, finance, and risk management.

## Section Plan

### 1. Hero
- Purpose: Frame capabilities as operational strengths, not marketing claims.
- Content: concise capability headline, supporting copy, CTA.

### 2. Capability Grid
- Purpose: Route into each detailed capability.
- Content: origination, trade finance, logistics, QA, risk management, market intelligence.

### 3. Process Timeline
- Purpose: Show how capabilities connect through the commercial lifecycle.
- Content: sourcing to delivery sequence.

### 4. Proof Of Execution
- Purpose: Reinforce trust.
- Content: metrics, corridor highlights, documentation standards, response expectations.

### 5. Industry And Sector Fit
- Purpose: Connect capabilities back to business use cases.
- Content: industries served and related product sectors.

### 6. CTA Band
- Purpose: Convert high-intent visitors.
- Content: request conversation, sourcing CTA, partner CTA.

## State Coverage

- Default: capability-led hub.
- Loading: capability-card skeletons.
- Empty: show core capability list and direct contact CTA.
- Success: not applicable.
- Error: degrade to high-level capability summary.
