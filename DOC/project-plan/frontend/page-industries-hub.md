# Industries Hub Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/industries`
- Template type: static plus CMS-fed hub
- Primary audiences: buyers, strategic partners
- Primary CTA: Explore Industry Solutions
- Secondary CTA: Contact Trade Desk

## Goal

Translate commodity and capability breadth into industry-specific relevance so visitors can quickly see how the company fits their market.

## Section Plan

### 1. Hub Hero
- Purpose: Frame the site around industries served, not just product supply.
- Content: industry overview, sectors supported, value proposition.

### 2. Industry Grid
- Purpose: Route into industry-specific pages.
- Content: food and feed, energy and utilities, agriculture inputs, manufacturing and industrial buyers.

### 3. Commodity-To-Industry Map
- Purpose: Show how products connect to use cases.
- Content: matrix or grouped cards linking products and sectors.

### 4. Capability Fit
- Purpose: Reinforce why the company can serve each industry.
- Content: QA, logistics, financing, documentation, sourcing flexibility.

### 5. Proof And Insights
- Purpose: Add credibility.
- Content: case studies, insight links, trust references.

### 6. CTA Band
- Purpose: Move from exploration to inquiry.
- Content: request quote, sourcing request, partnership CTA.

## State Coverage

- Default: industry-led browsing hub.
- Loading: industry cards and proof-card skeletons.
- Empty: if no industries are published, fall back to sector pages and contact CTA.
- Success: not applicable.
- Error: degrade to static industry summary and route links.
