# Insight Article Template Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/insights/[slug]`
- Template type: dynamic CMS template
- Primary audiences: SEO visitors, buyers, suppliers
- Primary CTA: Subscribe For Market Updates
- Secondary CTA: Explore Relevant Products

## Goal

Deliver a high-value, readable market or educational article that also creates clear routes into products, markets, and conversion points.

## Section Plan

### 1. Article Header
- Purpose: Establish topic, credibility, and context.
- Content: title, summary, author, publish date, topic tags.

### 2. Executive Summary
- Purpose: Support busy B2B readers.
- Content: key takeaways or bullet summary.

### 3. Article Body
- Purpose: Present structured insight.
- Content: rich-text blocks, charts, callouts, pull quotes.

### 4. Related Commercial Context
- Purpose: Connect content to services or products.
- Content: relevant product links, market links, capability references.

### 5. Related Articles
- Purpose: Increase depth and time-on-site.
- Content: three to four related insight cards.

### 6. Subscription And CTA Footer
- Purpose: Convert informed readers.
- Content: subscribe form, contact CTA, product CTA.

## State Coverage

- Default: full article template.
- Loading: SSR or streaming skeleton for hero and body blocks.
- Empty: not applicable.
- Success: subscription success feedback.
- Error: article unavailable state with back-to-insights and search CTA.
