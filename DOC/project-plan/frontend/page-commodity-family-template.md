# Commodity Family Template Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/products/[sector]/[family]`
- Template type: dynamic CMS template
- Primary audiences: buyers, suppliers
- Primary CTA: View Product Specifications
- Secondary CTA: Request a Quote

## Goal

Organize one commodity family in a way that supports comparison, discovery, and qualification for real procurement conversations.

## Section Plan

### 1. Family Hero
- Purpose: Define the family and its commercial role.
- Content: family summary, core applications, key buying criteria.

### 2. Product Navigator
- Purpose: Show all child products or grades.
- Content: product cards, quick facts, destination use tags.

### 3. Comparison Or Filter Layer
- Purpose: Help users narrow by grade, origin, packaging, or application.
- Content: lightweight filter controls and comparison cues.

### 4. Origin Options
- Purpose: Show supply choices and trade flexibility.
- Content: source markets, lead times, or shipping notes.

### 5. Industry Fit
- Purpose: Connect products to sectors and use cases.
- Content: buyer profiles and application summaries.

### 6. Supporting Resources
- Purpose: Reinforce understanding.
- Content: spec guides, FAQ, related insight articles.

### 7. CTA Zone
- Purpose: Convert high-intent visitors.
- Content: quote CTA, sourcing CTA, contact CTA.

## State Coverage

- Default: filterable family overview.
- Loading: filter shell and product-card skeletons.
- Empty: explain that no live child products are published and offer direct sourcing CTA.
- Success: not applicable.
- Error: degrade to static family description and inquiry CTA.
