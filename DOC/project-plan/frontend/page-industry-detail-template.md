# Industry Detail Template Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/industries/[slug]`
- Template type: dynamic CMS template
- Primary audiences: buyers, procurement teams
- Primary CTA: Request a Quote
- Secondary CTA: Explore Relevant Products

## Goal

Show how the company serves one industry through the right products, delivery model, documentation standards, and commercial understanding.

## Section Plan

### 1. Industry Hero
- Purpose: Define the industry lens and business context.
- Content: industry summary, headline challenges, demand profile.

### 2. Relevant Commodities
- Purpose: Connect the industry to specific product families.
- Content: featured commodities and product links.

### 3. Typical Supply Challenges
- Purpose: Demonstrate practical understanding.
- Content: volatility, timing, compliance, quality, logistics concerns.

### 4. Capability Fit
- Purpose: Show how the company solves those challenges.
- Content: sourcing, QA, finance, route planning, documentation support.

### 5. Proof Section
- Purpose: Add industry-specific credibility.
- Content: case study excerpts, relevant insights, corridor examples.

### 6. Conversion Block
- Purpose: Capture industry-aligned leads.
- Content: request quote, sourcing CTA, speak with specialist CTA.

## State Coverage

- Default: industry-specific narrative.
- Loading: hero and product-card skeletons.
- Empty: if product links are missing, show industry summary and direct contact CTA.
- Success: not applicable.
- Error: degrade gracefully to a static overview and route back to industries hub.
