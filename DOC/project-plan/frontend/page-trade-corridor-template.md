# Trade Corridor Template Page

## Metadata

- Route: `/markets/corridors/[slug]`
- Template type: dynamic CMS template
- Primary audiences: buyers, partners, logistics evaluators
- Primary CTA: Discuss This Trade Corridor
- Secondary CTA: View Related Markets

## Goal

Use corridor-specific detail to differentiate the brand through operational transparency and regional fluency.

## Section Plan

### 1. Hero
- Purpose: Name the corridor and its business relevance.
- Content: origin, destination, commodity focus, corridor summary.

### 2. Route Snapshot
- Purpose: Make the route concrete.
- Content: lead-time ranges, transit model, mode assumptions.

### 3. Documentation And Compliance
- Purpose: Show operational competence.
- Content: typical documents, customs or inspection notes, handoff requirements.

### 4. Risk Considerations
- Purpose: Demonstrate professionalism.
- Content: seasonality, congestion, price volatility, contingency thinking.

### 5. Related Markets And Products
- Purpose: Connect corridor detail back to discovery.
- Content: origin pages, destination pages, relevant products.

### 6. CTA Block
- Purpose: Convert route-specific interest.
- Content: contact specialist, request quote, sourcing CTA.

## State Coverage

- Default: corridor-led operational page.
- Loading: corridor-metric skeletons.
- Empty: show simplified route summary and direct support CTA.
- Success: not applicable.
- Error: degrade to related market pages and contact info.
